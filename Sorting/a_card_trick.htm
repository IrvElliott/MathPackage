A CARD TRICK

This trick requires a deck of cards that, when placed face-down, has been sorted so that the top four cards are aces, the next are four twos, etc. through four kings on the bottom. There should be 52 cards; no joker! The magician then holds the deck sideways in one hand and says that he/she is going to shuffle them. Next, the magician does several false cuts by taking about half the back side of the deck from the top, sliding it over then underneath and placing it underneath in the back. Thus, the original order is preserved.

Next, the magician says, "Now I am going to re-arrange the deck!" and blows on the cards. Next, starting with the top card, he repeatedly deals a card onto a table and spells one letter for each card, as shown in the table below. On each letter except the last letter of each line, he deals a card face down and calls out the letter. On the last letter of each line, he deals the card face-up and loudly calls out the last letter of the line.
